About JD Property Solutions

JD Property Solutions was founded with a simple goal — to provide Oklahoma City property owners and managers with a reliable, professional source for high-quality pool, concrete, and site improvement work.

We operate with a project-management model that allows us to match each job with the most skilled and experienced specialists in that trade. From concrete finishing and drainage correction to pool renovation and surface restoration, our trusted network of seasoned crews ensures every phase is done to commercial standards — safely, efficiently, and on schedule.

Every project is personally managed and quality-checked by James Durocher — Founder & Project Director, ensuring clear communication and consistent results.

Whether you manage an apartment community, commercial facility, or residential development, JD Property Solutions provides the leadership, organization, and field experience to get the job done right — the first time.
